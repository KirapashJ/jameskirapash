Who I Am

Educator. Developer.
Innovator.

I am James Leyian Kirapash — a passionate educator, self-taught programmer, and lifelong technology enthusiast based in Thika, Kenya. I hold a Bachelor of Education (Arts) from Africa International University and am currently a teacher at CARTA OAKHILL SCHOOL, where I inspire the next generation in Mathematics, Computer Studies, Business Studies, and Pre-Technical Studies.

Beyond the classroom, I build software, explore artificial intelligence, trade cryptocurrency, and chase the frontier of what technology can do for Africa. My mission is to fuse great education with powerful technology — and to prove that African innovators belong at the world's highest tables.

My long-term ambition is to become one of Africa's leading software engineers and technology entrepreneurs, creating platforms that open doors to education and financial freedom across the continent.
